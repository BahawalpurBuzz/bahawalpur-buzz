Bahawalpur Bookfair 2025

Date: Friday – Sunday, May 24-27, 2025

Time: 10:00 AM – 8:00 PM daily

Venue: The Central Library, Bahawalpur

Step into a world of words at the Bahawalpur Bookfair 2025! A 3-day celebration featuring book launches, author meetups, storytelling sessions, and massive discounts on thousands of titles across genres. Whether you love fiction, poetry, science, or kids' books — this event has something for everyone!
