Bahawalpur Food Festival 2025

Date: Saturday & Sunday, May 16-17, 2025

Time: 12:00 PM – 11:00 PM

Venue: Gulzar-e-Sadiq Park, Bahawalpur

Indulge in the best of Bahawalpur’s street food, desi flavors, international bites, and sweet treats at the grand Food Festival 2025! Live cooking stations, music, food challenges, and celebrity chefs — it's a full-on feast for your tastebuds and soul. Family-friendly, Insta-worthy, and flavor-packed!
