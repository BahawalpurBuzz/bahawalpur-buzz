Horse & Cattle Show 2025

Date: Friday – Sunday, May 20-21, 2025

Time: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM daily

Venue: Bahawalpur Polo Ground

Saddle up for the grand **Horse & Cattle Show 2025** in Bahawalpur! 🐄🐎 A traditional celebration featuring livestock beauty contests, horse dancing, polo exhibitions, bull races, camel parades, cultural performances, desi food, and handicrafts. Families, tourists, and locals alike gather to honor Punjab’s rich agricultural and rural roots.
